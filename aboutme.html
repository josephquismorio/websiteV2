about me

hi! my name is joseph "joey" quismorio, and i am currently a 20-year-old junior computer science student at texas a&m university at college station, with an interdisciplinary focus on russian language (привет друзья! меня зовут джоуи. здорово вас всех видеть!).

i primarily work in c++, python, and java, and try to hold a good foundation in html, css, and javascript. i've also had experience in scheme, a variation of lisp, as well as some assembly language and machine language in the form of nand2tetris projects. the tools i use include atom, visual studio code, pycharm, and netbeans.

outside of school, i enjoy listening to music (here's my spotify), making music on fl studio and ableton (here's my inactive soundcloud), watching as many movies as i can (sorry for all these account plugs... here's my letterboxd), reading (i'm currently reading infinite jest by david foster wallace - i don't think i'll need to update this for a while), and running my radio show on kanm's student radio (which you can listen to here on fridays at 2 pm!).

even in my youth, i've had a strong appreciation for and interest in all forms of art, including and especially the visual arts. from my experiences with studying both classic and modern aesthetics, i've come to embrace simplicity in content structure and design patterns, applying this to my field by emphasizing cleanliness and efficiency in how my code appears to others. i hope to, one day, fully bring my enthusiasm and passion for sleek and creative visual design into my work as a programmer, whether it be through the front or back end - i'll find a way regardless.
